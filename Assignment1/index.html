ANALOG FANTOM

Producer, MC, Guitarist, Button Masher

Analog Fantom was born out of boredom and desperation to hear new music. MCing in a train of thought style and using synthesizers, drum machines, effects pedals, guitars, and sampling various old records to create otherworldly sounds, Analog Fantom is all about pushing musical boundaries.
I've been making music for as long as I can remember and seems as natural to me as breathing air. From strumming wildly on my grandfather's Stratocaster as a toddler to figuring out the intricacies of an Akai MPC 2000, music is and always will be a part of me.
